🎵 About the Band

Djirection is a British-Irish rock band formed in London in 2023. The band consists of four talented musicians who came together with a shared vision of creating music that transcends boundaries and connects with audiences worldwide. Known for their energetic live performances and meaningful lyrics, Djirection has quickly become one of the most exciting new acts in the British music scene.
